Esperanza de Vida (Hope of Life) - Zacapa, Guatemala

In Guatemala’s remote mountain villages, families have no access to health care, or education and depend on subsistence farming for daily survival. As a result, malnutrition runs rampant, especially among young children.

Christian ministry Esperanza de Vida rescues starving children and nourishes them back to full health through nutrition, medical care and the love of Jesus. Please, help this life-saving ministry rescue these precious little ones!
