Elohim Christian School Feeding Program - Arequipa, Peru

Education is the key to escaping poverty, but when poor children are hungry and malnourished, they can’t learn. Your generous gift will help a Christian school in a destitute mountain community feed a nutritious lunch four times a week and breakfast one day a week to students from struggling families, jump-starting their school day.

Not only are these meals crucial to the children’s education experience. For many of them, it might be their only meal of the day. Help Elohim Christian School spread the Gospel in word and deed!

We meticulously honor the intent of our donors. 100% of all proceeds are restricted for the specific project for which they are raised. In the rare event that more funds are received than are required for a specific project, the additional funds will be used to meet the most urgent needs of the poor.

All donations made to Cross International are tax deductible and receipts will be provided. If you choose to contribute without seeking additional information, please remember to include the project name and number with your gift. Thank you and may God bless you!
