Children’s Club of Solino - Port-au-Prince, Haiti

"Restavek" is the Creole term for a child servant. More than 300,000 restavek children live far from their homes with host families who often subject them to negligence and abuse. Because they are kept out of school to do chores, most also remain illiterate.

That’s why Cross International supports the Children’s Club of Solino, a Christian organization that provides a lasting hope to some of Port-au-Prince’s most desperate children. Operated by a Christian network of pastors, this Sunday afternoon and summer program improves the lives of Haitian children – most of whom are living as restaveks and are sadly lacking in educational and social opportunities. Your gift through Cross International will give these young people a bright and hopeful future.
