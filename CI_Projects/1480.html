Sanctuary Child Development Center - Accra, Ghana

With sharp pangs of hunger burning in their bellies, it’s hard for poor children to concentrate on anything else. Hunger has become a particularly severe problem in the African nation of Ghana where roughly 1 in 10 children need to repeat a school year due to undernutrition. Eager young minds need nutrition to grow and develop.

The Sanctuary Child Development Center in Accra, Ghana, is a blessing to the hungry schoolchildren who attend and receive nourishment for both their bodies and minds, but it desperately needs your help and support to continue its ministry to our littlest brothers and sisters. Please make a generous contribution — it will be an answer to a hungry child’s prayers!
