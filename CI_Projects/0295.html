School of the Good Sower - Port-au-Prince, Haiti

This project will help educate orphans and special needs children living at a ministry center called Hope Village, run by Gladys Thomas, a Haitian-born, U.S.-trained nurse and Christian missionary. It will also serve the needy children from the surrounding neighborhoods.

This brand-new Port-au-Prince campus is divided into a primary school (pre-K, grades 1-6) for students and a special education school for at least 30 students, with curriculum devised in accordance with Haitian Ministry of Education standards. Your gift to School of the Good Sower will help provide salaries for teachers and staff, as well as educational supplies and operational expenses, to cover a period of 18 months.
