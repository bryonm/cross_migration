The Ursuline Sisters provide individually tailored school scholarships for 52 disadvantaged children living at St. Ann’s Home, a shelter for abused, neglected and abandoned girls.

FULLY FUNDED

Home for Girls Scholarship - Georgetown, Guyana

At St. Ann’s Home, providing a quality education is a top priority because many of the girls who come to the home are behind academically for their age and need special attention. That’s why Sr. Leonie De Costa, the Ursuline nun who operates St Ann’s, tries to send each girl to a handpicked school that best fits her developmental needs.

Cross Catholic Outreach is committed to providing scholarships that will allow these girls to have this tremendous educational opportunity. But we cannot do this without your help. This year, we hope you’ll prayerfully consider supporting this much-needed project, so these vulnerable young girls will have an otherwise inaccessible opportunity for learning and self-advancement.

 

We meticulously honor the intent of our donors. 100% of all proceeds are restricted for the specific project for which they are raised. In the rare event that more funds are received than are required for a specific project, the additional funds will be used to meet the most urgent needs of the poor.

All donations made to Cross Catholic Outreach are tax deductible and receipts will be provided. If you choose to contribute without seeking additional information, please remember to include the project name and number with your gift. Thank you and may God bless you!

For more information, e-mail us at info@catholic.org and include the project number in the correspondence. Thank you!
