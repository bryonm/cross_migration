Working Boy's Center

Quito, Ecuador
Project 0709

Provide daily meals for the younger siblings of working children who attend the Working Boys’ Center.

 

Working Boy's Center - Quito, Ecuador

Many families in Ecuador are so poor they depend on their children to shine shoes for money to buy food. Since the boys work all day long, they can’t go to school. But without an education, they will never find better jobs and get out of poverty. The Working Boys’ Center is helping to fill this gap by providing meals, literacy classes and job skills training. In addition, the boys and girls and their families have access to health care, housing assistance and spiritual nourishment.

Cross Catholic Outreach helps cover the cost of food, so the families can spend less time worrying about getting enough to eat and more time getting an education.
