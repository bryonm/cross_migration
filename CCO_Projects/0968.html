Food for Vulnerable Young Girls - Namaacha, Mozambique

In Mozambique, the HIV/AIDS pandemic has created a generation of orphans who face hunger, poverty, exploitation and possible HIV/AIDS infection. You can help orphaned and vulnerable girls escape this debilitating poverty by providing food to the children at Namaacha Girls’ Boarding Home operated by the Franciscan Missionaries of Mary.

The gift of a simple meal can change the future for a young girl desperate for hope. Please help us meet this need today!

We meticulously honor the intent of our donors. 100% of all proceeds are restricted for the specific project for which they are raised. In the rare event that more funds are received than are required for a specific project, the additional funds will be used to meet the most urgent needs of the poor.

All donations made to Cross Catholic Outreach are tax deductible and receipts will be provided. If you choose to contribute without seeking additional information, please remember to include the project name and number with your gift. Thank you and may God bless you!

For more information, e-mail us at info@catholic.org and include the project number in the correspondence. Thank you!
