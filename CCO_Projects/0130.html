Dagama Handicapped Student Meals

Project 0130

This project provides daily meals for about 180 physically disabled children at a Catholic boarding school.

 

Dagama Handicapped Student Meals - Luanshya, Zambia

The Franciscan Missionary Sisters of Assisi are providing an education for about 140 boarding students and 40 day students at the Dagama School for Physically Disabled Children. These children are stigmatized by their society because of their handicaps such as cerebral palsy, amputations and congenital deformities. But at Dagama, they receive the care and attention they need.

Your gift to Cross Catholic Outreach will help feed the students three nutritious meals a day – something they often go without at home because their families are too poor to provide for their basic needs.
