Feeding Program for Poor Boarding Students - Cotahuasi and Alca, Peru

For poor families in rural Peru, going to school requires an arduous walk from their mountain villages to the nearest big town. It can take several hours. As a result, many children stay home and never learn to read or write. The parents in these remote villages want a better life for their children, and Fr. Adrian Codevilla of the Order of the Incarnate Word has responded by offering very low-cost room and board near the schools.

At the mission’s Catholic boarding homes, the students do homework, receive tutoring, and are aided in their moral and spiritual formation, while receiving three nutritious meals a day. A gift from you will supplement the feeding program, and provide a home away from home for at least 100 students who are depending on our help.
