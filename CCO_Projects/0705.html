Namaacha Children’s Center - Namaacha, Mozambique

Mozambique is a country devastated by poverty and the HIV/AIDS pandemic. In this toxic environment, children beg on the streets for their next meal, face a high risk of abuse and often drop out of school. The Franciscan Missionaries of Mary offer a better life for these children at the Namaacha Children's Center day care and afterschool program.

Your gift will help keep kids in school by providing food, tutoring, educational materials and other expenses.

We meticulously honor the intent of our donors. 100% of all proceeds are restricted for the specific project for which they are raised. All donations made to Cross Catholic Outreach are tax deductible and receipts will be provided. If you choose to contribute without seeking additional information, please remember to include the project name and number with your gift. Thank you and may God bless you!
