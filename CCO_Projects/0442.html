Baraka Catholic Kindergarten

Ewuaso Kedong, Kenya
Project 0442

This project provides food and educational support for young children at Ewuaso Kedong Baraka Catholic Kindergarten.

 

Education for the Poor - Worldwide

In Kenya, a severe drought has devastated families of the Maasai tribe, who rely on their livestock for nourishment and income. Poor parents have trouble finding food for their children, much less sending them to school.

Thankfully, Baraka Catholic Kindergarten provides an affordable option for early education. Students not only learn how to read and write, but they receive physical nourishment for their bodies and spiritual sustenance for their souls. With your help, Cross Catholic Outreach will fund meals, salaries and operational costs.
