Meals for Poor Students - La Paz, Bolivia

You can help provide daily breakfast for university students attending Carmen Pampa University in La Paz. Bolivia is South America's poorest country, and these students come from the most impoverished rural areas and urban slums. Due to their poverty, many come to class without eating or getting the proper nutrition.

With your help, we can ensure that these students continue to receive a healthy breakfast so they have energy to succeed in school!
