Chisombezi Deafblind Centre - Limbe, Malawi

Deaf and blind children in Malawi are often considered a curse and neglected by their families and communities. The Chisombezi Deafblind Centre cares for these precious children and teaches them life and communication skills. Parents are also trained to care for their deaf and blind children and accept them as gifts from God.

Just $22,000 supports the Deafblind Centre, enabling a team of dedicated Catholic nuns to provide a quality education, nutritious daily meals, and a positive living experience for all the children in their care. Join us and help show these children the love and acceptance they desperately need.
